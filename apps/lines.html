Lines! is like a classic screensaver. Run the app, then place your iPhone, iPod touch or iPad upright on the table. Or just hold it in your hand. Watch as the lines move and bounce off the walls, as they smoothly transition between multiple colors. It's really pretty mesmerizing. Hope you enjoy it!

Lines! was the first app that I developed and released on the App Store on September 10, 2010 for iOS 3, with several thousand downloads its first weekend of release. It's based on code that I wrote many years ago, and is my Hello World app for graphics!

Lines! has been updated to support the latest iPhones, iPads, Apple Watch and TV.
